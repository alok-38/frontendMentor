What your BMI result means

A BMI range of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered a healthy weight. Maintaining a healthy weight may lower your chances of health issues such as obesity and type 2 diabetes.

Healthy eating

Promotes weight control, disease prevention, and improved digestion.

Regular exercise

Improves fitness, mood, and reduces disease risk.

Adequate sleep

Enhances recovery, mental clarity, and overall wellness.

Limitations of BMI

BMI is a useful guideline but may not apply equally to all individuals.

Gender

Body composition differs across genders and ages.

Age

Older adults may have more body fat despite normal BMI.

Muscle

High muscle mass can skew BMI results.

Pregnancy

BMI is not suitable during pregnancy.

Race

Health risks may vary across ethnic groups.
